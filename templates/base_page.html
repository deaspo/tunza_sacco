About Tunza Sacco Society

Tunza Vision.

To be a well-managed Co-operative society capable of Economically and Socially empowering its' members.

Tunza Mission.

To provide competitive, innovative profitable and sustainable savings and loan products and quality customer service through visionary leadership and upholding cooperative values.

Tunza Goal.

To enhance/improve and increase savings through membership and offer wide range of loan products and services in a sustainable manner.

Tunza Sacco Society Sacco.

Tunza is a voluntary financial scheme and service organization through which members make savings and in return have access to loan facilities at considerably low interest rates. Tunza is a Swahili word for “CARE”, and it strives to assist its members develop just the way CARE does while uplifting the living standards of the communities it works with. Tunza society is constantly in pursuit of meeting its objectives by improving the well being of all its members.
